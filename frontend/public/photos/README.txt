The home page carousel uses the PHOTOS folder at the project root:
  C:\Users\karan\gift-mart\PHOTOS

Put your images there as: img-1.jpg, img-2.jpg, img-3.jpg, img-4.jpg, img-5.jpg (or more).
They are served at /PHOTOS/ and the carousel loads them automatically.

You can also put images here in public/photos/ (URLs /photos/...) if you prefer.
